======================================================================
MANTEL-HAENSZEL DIF VALIDATION OF LORD'S-χ² SELECTED ANCHORS
======================================================================

Purpose: Cross-validate Lord's-χ²-selected anchors against MH, a
non-parametric DIF detector that does not require an IRT-calibrated
scale.

Anchors evaluated     : 39
Non-anchors evaluated : 236
Languages (vs English): 9  (ar, bn, it, jv, ko, sw, th, vi, zh)

----------------------------------------------------------------------
HEADLINE: rank correlation with Lord's χ²
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Spearman ρ = +0.235  (n = 157, p = 3.01e-03)
  Positive ρ means both detectors rank the same items as more / less
  DIF-prone, despite MH having no shared scale assumption with Lord's.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
CAVEAT
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  MH primarily detects UNIFORM DIF (consistent advantage at all ability
  levels). Non-uniform DIF — which parallels the 2PL discrimination term
  in our model — is not fully covered. See logreg_dif_anchor_validation.py
  for a complementary check that handles both.

Convergent corroboration from an independent method that does not depend
on IRT scale calibration.
